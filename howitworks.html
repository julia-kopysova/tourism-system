Steps for success

Choose the type of card

Open page "Price" and read carefully information about types of card, their highlights. In order to add a selected card to cart, you should sign up and press "Add to basket".

Pay

If you add all desired cards, check one more time your data and make payment using PayPal. Different changes after payment is impossible, so if you spotted a mistake, delete this card from cart and put again.

Show the card at the entrance

For convenience, you can take a screenshot or open the web-application on the device. It is imperative that you have your ID with the data that is written on the card. Enjoy your trip with the Shocard!

Find the card

In case of successful payment, you'll be able to see your cards in "Account" page. Cards, which were expired, display in history block. Also you can find your statistics and personal data there.

Fill the form

After you pressed the button, you'll see the form. Please specify the person, who will use this card. Upon entering the attraction, the cardholder must show ID.
