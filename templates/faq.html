FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Benefits

What are the main benefits of The Shocard?
You get the opportunity to visit all Kharkiv attractions at affordable prices, paying for the purchase only once.
Does The Shocard come with a guidebook?
In the description of each attraction there is an address with a link to Google Map.
How much can we save thanks to Shocard?
It depends on the number of days and places visited.

Savings

How can I find out how much credit I have left on my Shocard?
If you wish to confirm the outstanding credit on your London Pass then you should call our customer service team on +380 500 424 767.

Attractions

Does the pass include all of Kharkiv`s attractions?
The Shocard features over 80 top attractions. Check out our complete list of top Shocard attractions you can access with a Shocard Pass.
Are the attractions accessible all year round?
The majority of attractions included on Shocard are open all year round with just a few days of closure, usually around Christmas.
Can i visit the same attraction twice with my Shocard?
You can only visit each attraction included on the Shocard once. Repeat entry is not allowed.

Transport

Can I buy the Shocard with travel anywhere?
No, the Shocard with Travel can only be purchased online in advance.

Validity

How does The Shocard work?
Show Shocard at the entrance or ticket office of any attraction included, they will scan the card and give you entrance at no further cost. Show your card and head straight inside - it's as easy as that!
What happens if I change the date of my trip to Kharkiv?
Your Shocard (as well as your email confirmation voucher if you planned to collect your order in Kharkiv) is valid for one year from the date of purchase.

Purchase

How much does The Shocard cost?
The cost of a Shocard varies depending on the duration, pass type.
Can I buy The Shocard for a group?
We don't have any restrictions when purchasing through the website, however, the Shocard is not designed for large groups of travellers.
Why haven't I received my email confirmation?
Your email confirmation should normally arrive in your inbox a few minutes after completing a transaction/purchase. If you haven't received an email confirmation then we recommend you check your spam filter or junk folder.
